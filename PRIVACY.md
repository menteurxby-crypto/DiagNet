# DiagNet Privacy Policy

## Effective Date: March 26, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to DiagNet ("the App"). This privacy policy is designed to inform you about how the App collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information, as well as your rights regarding this information.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Ad-Related Data

- **Device Identifiers**: When using the free version of the App, we may collect device identifiers (such as IDFA) for ad targeting and performance evaluation.
- **Ad Interaction Data**: We collect information about your interactions with ads, such as ad clicks and viewing duration, to optimize ad delivery effectiveness.
- **Location Data**: With your authorization, we may collect approximate location information to provide more relevant ad content.

### 2.2 Other Data

- **Subscription Information**: When you purchase a subscription, we collect necessary purchase information, such as transaction ID and purchase date, to manage subscription status.
- **Feedback Data**: When you submit feedback, we collect the information you provide to improve App services.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Providing Services

- To process subscription transactions and manage subscription status.
- To respond to user feedback and support requests.

### 3.2 Ad Services

- To provide personalized ads using the collected data.
- To evaluate ad effectiveness and optimize ad delivery strategies.
- To share necessary data with advertising partners to provide ad services.

## 4. Information Sharing

### 4.1 Third-Party Service Providers

- **Advertising Partners**: We partner with advertising service providers such as Google AdMob and share necessary data to provide ad services. These partners' privacy policies may differ from this policy, and we recommend that you review their privacy policies.
- **Payment Processors**: When you make a subscription purchase, we share necessary transaction information with payment processors to complete the payment process.

### 4.2 Legal Requirements

- We may disclose your information if required by law, to comply with legal regulations, respond to legal proceedings, or protect our rights and interests.

## 5. Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: All network test data (such as ping, traceroute, port scan results, etc.) from the App is stored locally on your device and will not be uploaded to any server or cloud.
- **No Backend Services**: The App does not have any backend services or servers; all functions run locally on your device.

## 6. Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your information:

- Implement data encryption and access control measures.
- Restrict access to personal information to authorized personnel only.
- Regularly review and update security measures to address new security threats.

## 7. Your Rights

### 7.1 Accessing and Managing Your Information

- You can view and manage network test history data stored on your device.
- You can choose whether to authorize the App to access your device identifiers and location information.

### 7.2 Opting Out of Ad Tracking

- You can choose to limit ad tracking in your device settings to reduce personalized ads.
- You can remove ads by purchasing a subscription, thereby reducing data collection.

### 7.3 Data Deletion

- You can delete network test history data stored on your device.
- If you wish to delete other personal information we store, please contact us.

## 8. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we update the policy, we will notify you within the App and update the effective date on this page. We recommend that you review this policy periodically to understand how we protect your information.

## 9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this privacy policy, please contact us through:

- Email: \[[menteur@gmail.com](mailto:contact@diagnet.app)]
- In-app feedback function

# DiagNet 隐私政策

## 生效日期：2026年3月26日

## 1. 简介

欢迎使用 DiagNet（"本应用"）。本隐私政策旨在向您说明本应用如何收集、使用、存储和保护您的个人信息，以及您对这些信息的权利。

## 2. 我们收集的信息

### 2.1 广告相关数据

- **设备标识符**：当您使用免费版本的应用时，我们可能会收集设备标识符（如 IDFA）用于广告定向和效果评估。
- **广告互动数据**：我们收集您与广告的互动情况，如广告点击、观看时长等，以优化广告投放效果。
- **位置数据**：在您授权的情况下，我们可能会收集大致位置信息，用于提供更相关的广告内容。

### 2.2 其他数据

- **订阅信息**：当您购买订阅时，我们会收集必要的购买信息，如交易ID、购买日期等，用于管理订阅状态。
- **反馈数据**：当您提交反馈时，我们会收集您提供的信息，以改进应用服务。

## 3. 信息使用方式

### 3.1 提供服务

- 用于处理订阅交易和管理订阅状态。
- 用于响应用户反馈和支持请求。

### 3.2 广告服务

- 使用收集的数据为您提供个性化广告。
- 评估广告效果，优化广告投放策略。
- 与广告合作伙伴共享必要的数据，以提供广告服务。

## 4. 信息共享

### 4.1 第三方服务提供商

- **广告合作伙伴**：我们与 Google AdMob 等广告服务提供商合作，共享必要的数据以提供广告服务。这些合作伙伴的隐私政策可能与本政策不同，我们建议您查看他们的隐私政策。
- **支付处理商**：当您进行订阅购买时，我们会与支付处理商共享必要的交易信息，以完成支付流程。

### 4.2 法律要求

- 在法律要求的情况下，我们可能会披露您的信息，以遵守法律法规、响应法律程序或保护我们的权利和利益。

## 5. 数据存储

- **本地存储**：本应用的所有网络测试数据（如 ping、traceroute、端口扫描结果等）均存储在您的设备本地，不会上传到任何服务器或云端。
- **无后台服务**：本应用没有任何后台服务或服务器，所有功能均在本地设备上运行。

## 6. 数据安全

我们采取以下措施保护您的信息：

- 实施数据加密和访问控制措施。
- 限制对个人信息的访问，仅授权人员可以访问。
- 定期审查和更新安全措施，以应对新的安全威胁。

## 7. 您的权利

### 7.1 访问和管理您的信息

- 您可以查看和管理存储在您设备上的网络测试历史数据。

### 7.2 选择退出广告跟踪

### 7.3 数据删除

- 您可以删除存储在您设备上的网络测试历史数据。
